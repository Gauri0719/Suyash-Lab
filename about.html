Welcome to Dr. Ajit Chavan's Pathology Lab

Established in 2010, Dr. Ajit Chavan's Pathology Lab has been committed to providing accurate and reliable diagnostic services. Our state-of-the-art laboratory is equipped with the latest technology to ensure precision in every test we conduct.

We pride ourselves on our team of highly qualified professionals who are dedicated to delivering exceptional care and diagnostics. From routine blood tests to advanced imaging services, our goal is to support patients and healthcare providers in achieving better health outcomes.
